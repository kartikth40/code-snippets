# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this a welcoming, inclusive space for everyone preparing for tech interviews — regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews
- Help others learn — we were all beginners once
- Give credit when using someone else's ideas or solutions
- Keep content focused on interview preparation

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Sharing copyrighted content from paid courses
- Posting solutions to active hiring challenges or contests
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to interview prep

## Enforcement

Violations may result in comments being removed or contributors being blocked. If you see an issue, open a report via GitHub Issues.

## Scope

This applies to all interactions in this repository — issues, pull requests, discussions, and comments.
