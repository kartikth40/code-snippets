
### Behavioral Deep Dive
- [ ] Finalize all 15-20 STAR stories
- [ ] Practice each story 3+ times
- [ ] Record yourself, watch for:
  - Filler words ("um", "like")
  - Going over 3 minutes
  - Not quantifying results
- [ ] Prepare for common questions:
  - [ ] "Tell me about yourself" (2 min)
  - [ ] "Why [Company]?" (1 min)
  - [ ] "What's your weakness?" (1.5 min)
  - [ ] "Tell me about a time you failed" (3 min)
  - [ ] "Where do you see yourself in 5 years?" (1 min)

### Review Strategy
1. Identify your 3 weakest DSA topics
2. Redo 5 problems from each topic
3. Identify your 2 weakest system designs
4. Re-design from scratch with timer (45 min each)
